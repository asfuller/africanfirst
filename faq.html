FAQ

Where do your products come from?

Our beautiful Afrikan imports are shipped directly from Accra, Ghana and created in Agona Nsaba. Everything is 100% handmade.



How do I place an order?

You can place an order on the website by adding the product to your shopping cart then proceed to checkout. You can also follow our Instagram page and order through there and pay through Cashapp (option only for those in the Atlanta area).



Payment and Shipping

You may pay through the site or through PayPal. A shipping fee will be included.



Secure Ordering & Payment Options

Our site is secure, having the green padlock on the left side of the address bar. This means that all information is encrypted and will not be shared with anyone. You can check out through our site or through Paypal.



Returns & Refunds

All items are non-returnable. Refunds are only distributed if the customer has not received their product within a certain amount of time and, in this case, email us the issue and we will get back with you promptly.
